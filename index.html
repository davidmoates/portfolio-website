My First Labs Project Website, Life Logger

Life Logger was my labs project. I worked with 3 other developer and a Team Lead to build on top of a life task manager app. Usually we would have a mix of Back-end, Front-end, UX, and iOS/Android Developer, yet we only had member that went through the Full-Stack Development Curriculum. We started with 8 developer counting our Team Lead but after a week or so into the project we lost 3 of our member due to different circumstances. Most Labs teams have anywhere between 8-15 people. We turned this into a postive by communicating well and developing a friendship with everyone in our group. We worked extremely well and efficiently though none of us were in the same state. We communicated through Zoom and Slack. Together we recreated the whole app on the front-end side. I created a new logo and remade the Create Task form as well as the Edit Task form.
